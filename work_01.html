AKSARA
Indonesian Low Carbon Development Planning and Monitoring Application Previously known as PEP ONLINE

A collaborative program between Bappenas and GIZ provides low carbon development planning tools for government users both at the national and sub-national levels.

Visit Website

The Features

We support the development from the beginning, both conceptual and technical. We also assist in capacity development progress and various events.
AKSARA main feature is to collect low carbon development activity across Indonesia and calculate its emission reduction, and this participative approach aims to achieve Indonesia emission reduction target both international agenda and RPJMN agenda.

Planning

All forms of planning require oversight and monitoring to gauge success and suitability. The monitoring module presents various facilities for central and regional governments in monitoring the implementation of ongoing low carbon development activities

Monitoring

Finally, any effective effort needs to be reported and communicated equally effectively to all stakeholders in Indonesia and to the international community. The reporting module provides automatic reporting comprehensive, organized and reliable information for various stakeholders on all low carbon development implementation in Indonesia
