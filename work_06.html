Waste Management Calculator tool

As guidance to take actions in reducing waste problems, the Government of Indonesia (GoI) established the national waste management strategy, targeting 30% of municipal waste reduction and 70% of waste management (the base year 2017), as well as 70% of reduction targets for ocean plastic by 2025. In 2021, the Government of Indonesia (GoI) issued the Ministry of Home Affairs Regulation No.7/2021 on Calculation of SWM Retribution Tariff

The Features

We support the development of digital online SWM cost and retribution calculators placed in the AKSARA platform. The digital calculator will be used as a decision making-supporting tool for local government to estimate their SWM cost and retribution tariff.
The Waste Management Calculator develops based on html and Java and uses various highchart charts for data visualization.

Planning waste infrastructure recommendations for district needs




The development is based on Regulation of the Minister of Home Affairs Number 7 of 2021 concerning Procedures for Calculation of Levy Tariffs in the Implementation of Waste Handling. We develop the calculation on HTML and Java with graph visualization using highchart.

Calculating waste retribution per type of user for district needs




The system calculate of the cost of the entire process of managing waste, from collection, transportation, provision and operation of waste processing infrastructure till end on the landfill. Based on that, the system also prepare the tariff recomendation based on the regulation
